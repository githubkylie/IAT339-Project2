Our printing and production are done by Park Communications, London, who are committed to minimising their environmental impact, and won Environmental Printer of the Year Award at the PrintWeek Awards 2017, and were a finalist for the award in 2018. With them, Another Escape prints in vegetable-based inks on FSC and EU Eco-label paper, and all printing plates used in magazine’s production are recycled after use, and all waste is disposed of responsibly.

Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Another Escape is a magazine about outdoor life and creative culture. Explore, innovate, and discover the small details of a happy life, with a gentle and humble attitude, to seek a positive and fulfilling source of life.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Target customers is the people who likes outdoor life and creation. They can be students, businessmen, housewives, and other people of any profession. Although they live in the reinforced concrete city and are lost in the electronic world, they have a yearning for nature and a passion for exploration.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Another Escape has the people who love nature and their passionate stories, and pretty pictures. Reader will have the inspiration and courage when they finish the magzine. They will feel like they just escape the city life in a monment, hide in the nature. This magazine can inspire readers, to make them have a dream.
