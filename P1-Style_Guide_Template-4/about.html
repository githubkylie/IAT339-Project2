Another Escape is an outdoor lifestyle, creative culture and sustainable living journal that celebrates the stories of passionate people inspired by nature. It is a lifestyle publication for those who are creative, curious and outdoorsy, that aims to inspire a connection with nature, considered ways of living, environmental stewardship and a sense of fulfilment in our everyday lives.

Our story

In 2012, Another Escape founders, Rachel Taylor and Jody Daunton, came together to create a high-quality lifestyle publication that inspires a connection to nature, considered ways of living, environmental stewardship and a sense of fulfilment in our everyday lives. Through our love for storytelling, and working alongside our small team and a talented network of contributors, we have crafted a product for a community of outdoorspeople who are creative, curious and adventurous. We believe that if we can foster a meaningful relationship with nature then we may be best equipped and most motivated to offer stewardship to our planet, especially now, at a time when it so desperately needs it.
